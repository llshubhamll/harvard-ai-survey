Boons and Banes

Researchers rate AI's productivity benefit highly (mean 8.2/10), use it most intensively for coding and editing, and cite hallucinations and cost as the biggest challenges.

Key finding: 65% of respondents rated AI's productivity benefit at 8 or above, and 29% gave it a perfect 10. Coding assistance is by far the most intense use case (score 2.74 / 3.0).

Access & Payment

A striking contrast: ChatGPT users mostly have institutional access, while Claude users predominantly pay out-of-pocket. Over half of Claude's users across all roles are self-funding.

Key finding: 54% of Claude users pay personally out-of-pocket vs. only 23% for ChatGPT. Meanwhile 65% of ChatGPT users have institutional or lab funding, compared to just 29% for Claude.
